About Jason

Jason has been tinting glass in South Orange County since 1989 — before many of his customers were born. He built his reputation the old-fashioned way: by doing the work himself, using only the best materials, and standing behind every install.

There are no crews, no subcontractors, no unskilled helpers. When you hire Jason's Glass Tint, Jason personally installs your film — every residential window, every commercial pane, every vehicle, every boat.

He uses premium-grade window film only. No good / better / best tiers. No cheap film dressed up with a big markup. Just quality film, properly installed, the first time.
