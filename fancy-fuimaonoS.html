Our Story

Where Culinary Art
Meets Pure Elegance

Born from a passion for extraordinary food and unforgettable nights, Fancy Fuimaono's is Newport Beach's most celebrated luxury dining destination. We believe that a truly remarkable meal is more than flavors — it's the shimmer of candlelight, the pulse of live music, and the company that makes every evening legendary.

Our menus are crafted from the finest seasonal ingredients, curated by a team with decades of Michelin-caliber experience. Every plate is a statement. Every visit, a memory you'll carry.

Chef Spotlight

The Artisan
Behind the Plate

Every dish is a conversation between the land, the sea, and the soul of this city.

Our Executive Chef brings over two decades of refined culinary mastery from the kitchens of Paris, Tokyo, and New York. Trained under Michelin-starred mentors and fueled by a lifelong obsession with peak-season ingredients, our chef approaches every service as a performance — where technique meets intuition, and tradition meets daring innovation.

At Fancy Fuimaono's, the menu changes seasonally, reflecting whatever the California coast and Central Valley have to offer at their absolute finest.

The Fuimaono's Experience

Dine. Dance. Remember.

At Fancy Fuimaono's, dinner is only the beginning. As the night deepens, our intimate dance floor awakens with live music from world-class performers. Lose yourself in the melody, in the moment, in the magic that only Newport Beach can offer.

Curated Wine Cellar

Over 200 labels spanning Burgundy, Napa, and beyond — each chosen to complement our seasonal menu perfectly.

Live Music & Dancing

Friday and Saturday evenings feature live jazz, Latin, and contemporary performances. The dance floor is yours from 9 PM.

Private Dining Rooms

Three intimate private suites for celebrations, corporate dinners, and proposals — each furnished with bespoke luxury.
